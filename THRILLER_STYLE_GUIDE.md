# THRILLER STYLE GUIDE

## Atmosphere
- Cold, isolation, storm, dark wood interiors
- Everything must feel heavy, slow, watching

## Language
- Strong verbs
- Minimal adjectives
- Sharp sensory cues
- Break paragraphs for tension

## Emotion
- Fear in microbeats
- Avoid internal lectures
- Show through body and breath

## Hooks
- Last line must shift meaning, reveal danger, or change expectation
