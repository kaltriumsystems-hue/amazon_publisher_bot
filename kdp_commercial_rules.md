# AMAZON KDP — COMMERCIAL READABILITY RULES

## Chapter Structure
- Aim for 600–1500 words per chapter.
- All chapters end with a micro-cliffhanger.
- First 10 chapters must be extremely tight — 80% of readers decide here.

## Pacing Rules
- No long paragraphs (>7 lines) on Kindle.
- Alternate internal + external tension.
- Use sensory grounding every 4–6 paragraphs.

## Hook Optimization
Hooks should be:
- visual
- emotional
- ominous
- curiosity-driven
- short (1–2 sentences max)

## Psychological Thriller Requirements
- unreliable perceptions
- emotional stakes
- claustrophobic spaces
- hyper-specific sensory detail
- quiet menace over loud violence
- atmospheric tension

## KENP Optimization
- Fast readability
- Crisp chapter endings
- Strong retention pacing

## Character POV Rules
- one strong internal voice
- trauma-accurate reactions
- no melodrama
- no excessive exposition

## Dialogue Rules
- sparse
- purposeful
- emotionally weighted
