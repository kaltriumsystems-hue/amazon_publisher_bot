# 7-LAYER EDITING PIPELINE — THRILLER EDITION

## LAYER 1 — Story Logic & Continuity
- Ensure events follow naturally  
- No contradictions  
- Timeline consistent  
- Setting details stable  
- Keep emotional consequences aligned  

## LAYER 2 — Character Psychology
- Actions must align with trauma, past, patterns  
- Reactions must be emotionally logical  
- No out-of-character shifts  
- Avoid melodrama and emotional shortcuts  

## LAYER 3 — Tension & Suspense Engineering
- Gradual escalation  
- No dropped threads  
- Clear stakes  
- Atmosphere maintained (storm, lodge, cold)  
- Threat always present  

## LAYER 4 — Pacing Optimization
- Short paragraphs in tension scenes  
- Cut filler  
- Sharpen beats  
- Inner monologue: tight & purposeful  
- Scene transitions smooth but fast  

## LAYER 5 — Line Editing
- Improve clarity  
- Remove weak verbs  
- Shorten overlong sentences  
- Strengthen rhythm  
- Keep sensory detail sharp, minimal, precise  

## LAYER 6 — Voice Protection
- Emma: intimate, anxious, precise  
- Liam: guilt-heavy, reactive  
- Evan: cold, measured, eerily gentle  
- Jenna: bright masking panic  
- Do NOT flatten voices  

## LAYER 7 — Commercial Optimization
- Chapter hooks must hit hard  
- Ensure readability on Kindle  
- Emotional impact per scene  
- Alignment with top-selling psychological thrillers  
- Page-turner quality preserved
