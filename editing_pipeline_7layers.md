# 7-LAYER EDITING PIPELINE

## LAYER 1 — Story Continuity
Check:
- events line up  
- emotional reactions match earlier chapters  
- no contradictions  
- no time/place inconsistencies  

## LAYER 2 — Character Logic
Check that every action is:
- psychologically correct  
- aligned with motivation  
- consistent with trauma, patterns, fears  

## LAYER 3 — Suspense Structure
Questions:
- Is tension rising?  
- Is the reader pulled forward?  
- Does fear escalate logically?  
- Are stakes clear?  

## LAYER 4 — Pacing & Rhythm
Fix:
- too-long paragraphs  
- repetitive sentences  
- slow emotional loops  
- unclear beats  

## LAYER 5 — Line Editing
Improve:
- clarity  
- readability  
- natural flow  
- sentence-level precision  

## LAYER 6 — Voice Protection
Ensure:
- Emma's POV stays intimate  
- tone stays thriller  
- NO AI patterns  
- NO overwritten sentences  

## LAYER 7 — Commercial Optimization
Check:
- hook  
- chapter length  
- emotional punch  
- market alignment (Amazon top sellers, psychological thriller)  

Deliver:
- revised chapter  
- notes (brief)  
- hook rating  
- publishability rating
